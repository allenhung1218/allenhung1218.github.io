Aug 23, 2021 @ The New York Times [Original Article]
by Paula Span

[sec. #1]

Learning your odds of eventually developing domentia - a pressing concern for many, especially those with a family history of it - requires medical testing and counseling. But what if everyday behavior, like overlooking a couple of credit card payments or habitually braking while driving, could foretell your risk? A spate of experiments is underway to explore that possibility, reflecting the growing awareness that the pathologies underlying dementia can begin years or even decades before symptoms emerge.
